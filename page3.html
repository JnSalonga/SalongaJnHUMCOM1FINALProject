Board of Trustees

The Board of Trustees of the University of the Cordilleras promotes the sound development of the institution within the functions prescribed for it, helping it to serve the country in a way that will complement the activities of the other institutions and aiding it to perform at a high level of excellence in every area of endeavor.





Executive Council

The Executive Council is consist of the President of the University, Vice President for Academic Affairs, Vice President for Administration, Vice President for Finance, Vice President for Research Development and Special Projects, Assistant Vice President for Corporate Communications, and Assistant Vice President for Management Information System.
